Flower Show at Curzon Park Mysore Dasara 2019 Flower Show will be organised by Mysore District Horticulture Society and the Department of Horticulture. The Dasara Flower Show at Curzon Park is one of the major attractions during the Mysore Dasara festivities. The venue of the Flower Show will be adorned with about 35,000 different kinds of plants bearing flowers and vegetables. Flower arrangement with dried flowers of various kinds will also feature in the show this time. Every year there would be specialized themes for the show.
